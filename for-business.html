About the edit

Shorts built to make the idea clear fast.

The goal is simple: turn a script into a video people understand quickly and keep watching.

Each edit is built on six pillars of retention:

  • Hook: Stopping the scroll immediately
  • Pacing: Maintaining engagement throughout
  • Captions: Enhancing clarity and rhythm
  • Visuals: Directing focus with intent
  • Sound: Adding polish and emphasis
  • Payoff: Closing with a clear takeaway

FAQ

Answers before you send a brief.

Use this section to decide what to send, what to expect, and which option fits your project.

Getting started

What should I send first?

Send your script or outline, voiceover if you have one, reference links, deadline, and the goal of the Short.

Voiceover

Can you work from only a voiceover?

Yes. A clean voiceover plus a few references is enough to plan the pacing, captions, and visuals.

Script help

Do you write scripts too?

I can tighten the hook, cut weak lines, restructure the flow, or suggest a stronger ending before editing.

Deliverables

What do I receive?

A ready-to-post vertical MP4 for YouTube Shorts. For batches, I can also keep the caption and pacing style consistent.

References

How should I send references?

Paste Shorts, Reels, TikToks, or brand examples that match the pacing, caption style, visual tone, or audience.

Timeline

How long does one Short take?

Most single Shorts take 2-4 days once the script, voiceover, and references are ready.

Revisions

Can I request changes?

Yes. Send focused notes on captions, timing, visuals, music, or any required message changes.

Best fit

Is this only for creators?

No. I also edit Shorts for freelancers, educators, founders, small businesses, and marketing teams.
